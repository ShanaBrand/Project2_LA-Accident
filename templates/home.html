los angeles. 2010 - present.
traffic collisions.

As everyone in Los Angeles knows, we have an abundance of cars and accidents. This project utilized car accident data from the City of Los Angeles to answer a few questions.

  • How frequent are car accidents and has this changed over the last several years?
  • Are accident victims more likely to be male or female?
  • Do victims more frequently fall into certain age ranges?
  • What are some of the accident hot spots in Los Angeles?
